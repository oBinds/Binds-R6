Features

ALL PAGES
Open Menu
L2+OPTIONS

Hold L2 and press Options to open the menu. UP/DOWN navigates categories, CROSS enters, CIRCLE goes back, LEFT/RIGHT D-Pad scrolls pages inside a category. All settings save automatically on close.

ANTI-RECOIL — ATK & DEF
Per-Gun Anti-Recoil
MENU ONLY

Every gun has its own Vertical and Horizontal recoil values saved permanently to the Zen. Switching guns on the same operator automatically loads the correct values for each — no manual adjustment needed. Tune in Anti-Recoil → AR Vertical / AR Horiz.

OP & GUN — ATK & DEF
Gun Selection
UP/DOWNbrowse✕ SELECT

Open the Gun page for your operator. Scroll UP/DOWN to browse all primaries and secondaries — browsing never changes values. Press ✕ on the gun you have equipped in-game. A * marks the active selection. Primary and secondary are selected independently. When you swap loadout mid-match, just re-select the new gun in the menu.

RAPID FIRE — ATK & DEF
Rapid Fire
MENU ONLY

Rapid fire for semi-automatic guns. Semi guns default ON — OTS-03, CAMRS, AR-15.50, D-50, 417, SDP 9mm, PMR90A2, TACIT .45, P226 MK 25, P9 etc. Full-auto guns default OFF. Toggle per slot and adjust RF Speed in the Rapid Fire pages. Use RF For All to override every gun at once.

MOVEMENT — ATK & DEF
Movement Suite
MENU ONLY

Full per-side movement features, each with its own speed slider.
Crouch Spam — rapid crouch/stand while firing  |  Strafe — automated side movement
Shaiko Lean — alternates L3/R3 while holding ADS+R2  |  Lean Spam — spams R1/L1
Tea Bag, Auto Lean, Prone Shot, Hip Lean, Inverted AR and more. ATK and DEF fully independent.

MISC — ATK & DEF
Stick Lean
L2+L3/L2+R3

Stick-click lean spam triggered by L2 + L3/R3.
Tap L2+L3 or L2+R3 — works as a completely normal single lean.
Hold L2+L3 past 250ms — continuously spams lean left (L3 on/off cycle).
Hold L2+R3 past 250ms — continuously spams lean right (R3 on/off cycle).
Speed uses Shaiko Spd setting. Enable in Misc → Stick Lean. ATK and DEF independent.

R6 MISCS — PAGE 58
Soft Triggers
MENU ONLY

Any touch of R2 or L2 — even the slightest press — instantly fires the trigger at 100%. Eliminates all trigger deadzone for the fastest possible ADS and fire response. ON by default. Toggle via R6 Miscs → Soft Triggers (accessible from the category screen with the Miscs button).

MISC — ATK & DEF
Misc Options
MENU ONLY

Per-side extras including RF For All, Skel Key RF, On Cams (recoil on operator cameras), Pin On Shot, Auto Scan, Dead Zone adjustment, Inverted AR, Abilities, LED colour control, and Stick Lean toggle.

Tips & Usage

  • First time setup: Clear the Zen's persistent memory before loading the script. This lets all the baked-in gun defaults load cleanly without old data interfering.
  • Selecting your gun every session: When you change your loadout in-game, open the menu, navigate to your op's Gun page and press ✕ on whichever gun you equipped. Values load instantly and save to the Zen — no need to do it again unless you swap guns.
  • Same gun = same value: MPX on Valkyrie and MPX on Tubarao share the same saved values. Tuning MPX once updates it across every operator that uses it.
  • Shaiko vs Stick Lean: Shaiko (Movement tab) spams both left and right lean alternating while ADS+shooting. Stick Lean (Misc tab) spams one direction only — hold L2+L3 for left, L2+R3 for right. Best for aggressive one-way peeking.
  • Rapid Fire is already tuned: All semi guns come with RF ON pre-set at the right speed. Full-auto guns come with RF OFF. Any changes you make in the menu save permanently to the Zen.
  • Soft Triggers are ON by default. Barely touching L2 or R2 fires instantly at 100%. Turn off in R6 Miscs if needed for any reason.
  • ATK and DEF are completely independent. Every feature — recoil, RF, movement speed, stick lean — can be configured differently per side. Changes on ATK never affect DEF.
  • Everything saves automatically when you close the menu. Next power cycle loads your exact settings back. No need to reconfigure each session.
